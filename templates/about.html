About SyncWave

SyncWave is a mood-driven music discovery web app that adapts recommendations based on how you feel over time.

Instead of static playlists, SyncWave tracks listening behavior, analyzes recent activity, and gently shifts recommendations to match your emotional patterns.

All data is stored locally in your browser. No accounts, no tracking, no data collection.

Built by Aryaman & Arshiya as a full-stack project using Flask, vanilla JavaScript, and modern UI principles.
